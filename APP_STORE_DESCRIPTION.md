## Description

Take control of your finances without giving up your privacy. Finance Tracker is a fast, beautiful, and completely local money manager — every cent you record stays on your phone. No accounts. No subscriptions. No one watching.

---

### 📊 Know Where You Stand, Instantly

Your **Dashboard** shows your real-time balance, total income, and total expenses the moment you open the app. A glanceable bar chart tracks your spending trend over the last 7 days or 8 weeks so you can spot patterns before they become problems.

---

### ✏️ Add Transactions in Seconds

Recording a transaction is fast and frictionless:
- Give it a title, pick income or expense, enter the amount
- Choose a date and add an optional note
- Attach a **receipt photo** — taken fresh with your camera or picked from your library — so you always have proof
- Categorise expenses under Housing, Food, Transport, Health, Insurance, or Other

No internet required. Everything saves instantly to your device.

---

### 🔍 Find Anything, Fast

The **Transactions** screen is built for real use:
- **Live search** across titles and notes as you type
- **Quick tabs** to jump between All, Income, and Expenses
- **Advanced filters** — by date range (Today, This Week, This Month, Last Month, This Year, or a custom range), expense category, amount range, and sort order (newest, oldest, highest, or lowest)
- **Grouped by date** with Today / Yesterday labels so you always know when things happened
- Tap the 🧾 receipt icon on any row to view the full receipt image without opening the transaction

---

### 📈 Understand Your Habits

The **Insights** screen turns your data into three interactive charts:

- **Income vs Expenses donut** — see at a glance whether you're ahead or behind
- **Expenses by Category donut** — find out where the money actually goes
- **Income, Expenses & Balance line chart** — track trends across weeks, months, or years with a single tap

---

### 🤖 AI-Powered Financial Advice

Tap **Get AI Suggestions** and Gemini — Google's AI — analyses your real transaction data and gives you three specific, actionable tips. Not generic advice. Suggestions that reference your actual spending. Free daily quota included.

---

---

### 🔒 Privacy First — Always

Finance Tracker is built on a single, non-negotiable principle: **your data never leaves your phone.**

- No account required — open the app and start
- No cloud sync — nothing is sent to any server
- All data stored in SQLite, locally on your device
- Receipt images compressed and saved only in your local storage
- Even the AI feature sends only a anonymised summary — never your raw data

---

### 🌙 Comfort for Every Condition

- **Dark Mode** and **Light Mode** — switch instantly from Settings, remembered across restarts
- Clean, modern design that's easy to read at a glance

---

### 💾 Your Data, Your Rules

Back up and restore your transactions at any time:
- **Export as JSON** — full backup for restoring to Finance Tracker on a new phone
- **Export as CSV** — open in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets
- **Import** — merge with existing data or replace it entirely

Switching phones? Export on the old one, import on the new one. Done in under a minute.
